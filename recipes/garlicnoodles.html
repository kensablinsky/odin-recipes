Garlic Noodles

Comes together very quickly, and has a punch of garlic and umami flavors

Ingredients

  • 0.5 lb (220g) Spaghetti noodles
  • 12 cloves garlic
  • 3 TBPS butter
  • 2-3 tsp soy sauce
  • 2-3 tsp fish sauce
  • 2-3 tsp oyster sauce
  • Pecorino or parmesan cheese, grated
  • Scallions (optional)
  • Bottarga, grated (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook pasta without salt until just under al dente
  2. Meanwhile, smash garlic in morter and pestle with a little salt to form a paste
  3. Add butter to pan udner medium low and melt
  4. Add garlic to pan, and cook but do not let brown
  5. Add a few tsp of soy sauce, fish sauce, and oyster sauce
  6. Add noodles to pan, including a small bit of the pasta water, and blend together
  7. Add more pasta water if too tight
  8. Plate, and add chopped scallions and grated bottarga (if using)
